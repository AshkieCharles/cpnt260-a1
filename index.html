Destiny

The Different Races and Factions

Destiny and Destiny 2 has several different opposing factions that makes up the forces of Darkness that humanity has to fight. The Fallen are group of space pirates, The Cabal are warmongering space rhinos, The Vex are time travelling robots, The Hive is an entire species devoted to killing and The Taken are a combination of all the previous enemies that follows the will of a God.
