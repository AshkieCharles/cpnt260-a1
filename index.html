Destiny

The Different Races and Factions

Destiny and Destiny 2 has several different opposing factions that makes up the forces of Darkness that humanity has to fight. The Fallen are group of space pirates, the Cabal are warmongering space rhinos, the Vex are time traveling robots, the Hive is an entire species devoted to killing and the Taken are a combination of all the previous enemies that follows the will of a God for they lost their own.

    Fallen
  • Fallen

    • The Fallen, also known as the Eliksni in their own tongue, are an alien race that looks similar to that of insects and are infamous for looting and raiding anything they get their claws on. Their civilization was once a mighty society that lived with the Traveler's protection and grace.
